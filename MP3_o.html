Welcome to Nikki Smalley's
Audacity Page

For this project, I used a keyboard to record a rendition of
Heart and Soul over a Rock 2 beat. Then I recorded an original
song to play over that track. I used the fade in and fade out
effects on the beat track and I used the high pass filter and
normalize effects on the voice track and set the gain to -10.
